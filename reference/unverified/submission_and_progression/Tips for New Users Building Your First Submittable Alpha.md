## 帖子正文

Starting on BRAIN can feel complex, but great Alphas come from simple, logical ideas. Begin small — understand price behavior, not just code.Focus on key metrics:Sharpe above 1.25,Turnover below 70%, andFitness above 1. These show consistency, efficiency, and realistic trading.Use smart settings:Delay1,Industry Neutralization,Decay ≈ 5, andTruncation ≈ 0.01. These stabilize results and control risk.After each simulation, study your PnL, note what improved, and adjust one variable at a time. Small refinements lead to big progress.Your first Alpha doesn’t need to be perfect—justclear, stable, and logical. The best quants think deeply, test patiently, and build step by step.
