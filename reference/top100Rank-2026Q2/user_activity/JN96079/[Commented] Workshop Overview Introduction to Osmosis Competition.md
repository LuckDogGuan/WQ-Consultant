### 评论 #5 (作者: DL51264, 时间: 5个月前)

Osmosis is basically a sandbox where you allocate points to your own alphas and see how they perform together under real costs and correlations. It’s more about learning allocation and signal interaction than just winning. Winners don’t directly boost Genius or combined scores, but strong Osmosis performance often reflects higher-quality alphas, which indirectly helps your overall profile and decision-making.
