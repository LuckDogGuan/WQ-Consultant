### 评论 #2 (作者: HN18962, 时间: 2个月前)

Hi SP61833, it sounds like you're wrestling with how to best capture the "Orthogonal HTVR Theme." The HTVR (High Turnover-Value Ratio) concept often implies something about the *predictability* of returns generated from high turnover, not just the turnover itself. Did you explore how the PnL realization might be related to the *rate* of turnover, perhaps looking at shorter lookback periods for PnL given the high turnover condition? It's a subtle distinction, but might be key to aligning with the "orthogonal" aspect.
